Individual Contributor Trap

You cannot be a good leader when you're busy. Unbusy yourself!

  • As a leader, your job is to see things that others cannot see.
  • Focus on vision (black box design) first
  • Can we combine organizational and technical work without being overwhelmed?
  • Yes: concentrate on HLAs!
  • NOTE: This does not necessarily mean you don't work hard/do overtime. Difference between swimming hard to avoid drowning and swimming hard to be ahead of the competition.
