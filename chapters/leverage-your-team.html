Change how the Team(s) Work

High-performing teams are...

aligned
autonomous
cross-functional
self-organizing and self-directed
small
stable (remember Tuckman?)
psychologically safe
undistracted and uninterrupted
moderately cognitively loaded
  • Aligned: everyone knows what the team is working on and why
  • Autonomous: the team can make decisions without asking for permission
  • Cross-functional: the team has all the skills necessary to deliver value (diversity)
  • Self-organizing: the team can decide how to work
  • Self-directed: the team choses its own scope
  • Psychologically safe: people feel safe to speak up and take risks
  • Moderately cognitively loaded: people are not overloaded with work and can focus on the task at hand

Great teams are grown, not hired

  • unfortunately management often rotates people, up-or-out is poison!

    "There is a strong correlation between the quality of the codebase and velocity."
    • The currency of technical debt is velocity. When we take out a debt, we have to pay it back with compound interest.
    • Technical debt is a tool for short-term gain. It can cause addiction and must be carefully managed.
