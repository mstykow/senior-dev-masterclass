  • Trust that the natural process of evolution will present you with new challenges

  • Inverse bus factor: how many need to get hit by a bus for the project to get back on track?

Exercise

Rank the following tools that affect employee motivation:

  • Support for making progress in the work
  • Recognition for good work
  • Incentives
  • Interpersonal support
  • Clear goals
  • 700 managers from around the world
  • only 5% ranked "making progress" first
  • most ranked "recognition" first, followed by "incentives"

2/5: Challenge
  • Delegate technical and non-technical work
  • Delegate tasks that maximize learning (trade-off with speed)
  • Delegate the most interesting work
  • Remember: your job is to make yourself unneeded by teaching people how to solve their own problems rather than solving them for them
  • Your ego will insist that you do. Your potential will insist that you delegate.

5/5: Build Trust

Trust is the product of:

  • Knowledge
  • Consistency
  • Authority
  • Confidence (mental/physical health, ability to manage stress/anxiety)
  • The company you keep
  • Charisma (energy, body language 55%, voice 38%, words 7%)
  • Communication skills
  • Your EQ (art of managing mind, thoughts, emotions, empathy)
  • Accountability (own your mistakes)
